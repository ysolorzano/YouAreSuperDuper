You Are Super Duper!


Come join us to lift up and lift off!

On November 21st, Super Duper is hosting an event for children battling cancer to be team captain to build and launch rockets! Each captain has the opportunity to be bring a team of one to two super duper sidekick friends who together will design, construct and launch kid-friendly rockets. Sidekicks can be anyone -- a friend, supporter, cousin, sister, brother, etc -- as long as they are between the ages of 5 and 10 years old.
